What is PaidNow?

PaidNow is a fintech platform enabling employees to access a portion of their earned wages instantly before payday. By integrating with employer payroll systems, PaidNow provides responsible Earned Wage Access without loans or interest, helping workers manage cash flow while improving employee wellbeing.

The PaidNow Story

Many employees work hard every day, yet still find themselves struggling before payday. Bills arrive at different times of the month. Unexpected expenses happen. But salaries are often locked into rigid monthly pay cycles.

The result is that millions of workers are forced to rely on expensive payday loans or short-term credit just to bridge the gap to their own earnings.

PaidNow was created after seeing colleagues face this exact problem. People were working and earning their income, yet they still couldn't access it when they actually needed it.

PaidNow is building the infrastructure for real-time pay, allowing employees to access a portion of the wages they have already earned—instantly, responsibly, and without loans or interest.

Because if you've already earned it, you should be able to access it.

How Earned Wage Access Works:

Earned wage access allows employees to withdraw a portion of their already-earned wages before payday. Unlike payday loans or credit, this is money you've already worked for, making it a responsible alternative to high-interest borrowing. PaidNow helps reduce financial stress, improve productivity, and support workforce wellbeing.
