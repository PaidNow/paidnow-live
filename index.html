What is PaidNow?

PaidNow is a South African fintech platform that gives employees instant access to wages they have already earned, before payday. It is not a loan. There is no interest. PaidNow integrates with employer payroll systems to calculate earned wages in real time and allows employees to withdraw a portion instantly.

The Problem PaidNow Solves

Millions of South African workers are paid monthly. But bills, emergencies, and everyday expenses do not wait for payday. The result is that workers turn to expensive payday loans, loan sharks, or credit cards just to access money they have already earned. PaidNow eliminates that gap entirely.

How PaidNow Works

PaidNow connects to an employer's payroll system. As an employee works, PaidNow tracks their earned wages in real time. When the employee needs funds, they open the PaidNow app, select how much of their earned wages they want to access, and the money is transferred instantly to their bank account. At the end of the pay cycle, the amount is reconciled automatically with payroll.

Is PaidNow a Loan?

No. PaidNow is not a loan, a credit product, or a cash advance. Employees are accessing wages they have already earned through their own work. There is no interest, no credit check, and no debt created. This is a fundamental difference from payday lenders and short-term credit providers.

PaidNow vs Payday Loans

Payday loans charge high interest rates and create debt. PaidNow charges no interest because it is not a loan. Payday loans require credit checks. PaidNow does not. Payday loans can trap workers in debt cycles. PaidNow simply gives workers access to money they have already earned.

Who is PaidNow For?

PaidNow is for any South African employee who is paid on a monthly or bi-weekly cycle and wants access to their earned wages before payday. It is also for employers who want to offer a meaningful financial wellness benefit to their workforce at no cost to the business.

Is PaidNow Free for Employers?

Yes. PaidNow is completely free for employers to offer as a benefit. There are no setup fees, no monthly costs, and no integration charges for the business. Employers simply connect their payroll system and PaidNow handles the rest.

Is PaidNow POPIA Compliant?

Yes. PaidNow is built to be fully compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). All user data is stored in South Africa on AWS infrastructure in the Cape Town region (af-south-1), ensuring data sovereignty and regulatory compliance.

What is Earned Wage Access?

Earned wage access (EWA) is a financial service that allows employees to access a portion of their already-earned wages before their scheduled payday. It is also referred to as on-demand pay, early wage access, or real-time pay. EWA is growing rapidly globally as an alternative to payday loans and short-term credit.

Earned Wage Access in South Africa

South Africa has one of the highest rates of over-indebtedness in the world. Monthly pay cycles leave workers financially vulnerable for weeks at a time. Earned wage access is a responsible, interest-free solution that addresses this structural problem. PaidNow is building the infrastructure to make earned wage access available to every South African employee.
