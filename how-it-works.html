How It Works

Access your earned wages in three simple steps

1

Your Employer Integrates with PaidNow

PaidNow securely connects with your employer's payroll system. This integration allows us to track your earned wages in real-time.

No cost to employers. Easy integration with any payroll system.

2

You Work and Earn

As you work each day, your earned wages accumulate in the PaidNow system. You can see exactly how much you've earned at any time.

Real-time tracking of your earned income.

3

Access Your Money Instantly

When you need funds before payday, simply request a portion of your already-earned wages through the PaidNow app. The money is transferred to your account instantly.

No loans. No interest. Just your money, when you need it.
