The PaidNow Story

Reimagining payday for the modern workforce

The Problem We Saw

Many employees work hard every day, yet still find themselves struggling before payday.

Bills arrive at different times of the month. Unexpected expenses happen. But salaries are often locked into rigid monthly pay cycles.

The result is that millions of workers are forced to rely on expensive payday loans or short-term credit just to bridge the gap to their own earnings.

Why We Started PaidNow

PaidNow was created after seeing colleagues face this exact problem.

People were working, earning their income, and still couldn't touch it when they needed it most.

The issue was never about earning enough. It was that the monthly pay cycle stopped making sense for how people actually live.

Our Solution

PaidNow is building the infrastructure for real-time pay. Employees can access a portion of wages they have already earned, instantly, without loans or interest.

If you've already earned it, you should be able to access it.

Our Mission

No waiting for payday to access money you've already worked for.

We're not offering loans. No interest. Just your money, when you need it.

Building for the Future

Real-time work deserves real-time pay.

We're building toward a world where financial stress doesn't hold back people who show up every day, and where payday is whenever you need it.
