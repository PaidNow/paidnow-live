The hidden cost of monthly pay cycles

Financial stress is the leading cause of absenteeism and presenteeism in South African workplaces. When employees can't bridge the gap between expenses and payday, productivity drops, turnover rises, and HR teams absorb the burden of ad-hoc salary advance requests.

The traditional response — payday loans and short-term credit — makes the problem worse. Employees take on debt at high interest rates to access money they've already earned. PaidNow eliminates this gap entirely, at a fraction of the cost of employee turnover.

Implementation

Four steps. No disruption to your existing processes.

1

Payroll integration

We connect to your existing payroll system via secure API. No changes to your pay cycle, processes, or reporting.

2

Employee enrolment

Staff download the PaidNow app and link to your organisation. Onboarding is self-service — no HR involvement required.

3

Real-time wage tracking

As employees work, their earned balance updates automatically. They can access a portion of earned wages at any point.

4

Automated reconciliation

At month-end, all withdrawals are reconciled with payroll automatically. Zero manual processing on your side.

This is not a loan

PaidNow is fundamentally different from payday lenders or salary advance products. Employees access wages they have already earned through their own work. There is no interest, no credit check, and no debt created.
