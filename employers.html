PaidNow for Employers

A zero-cost benefit your team will actually use

The cost of financial stress

78% of South African employees live payday to payday. Financial stress leads to absenteeism, low productivity, and high turnover — all of which cost your business money.

Salary advance requests burden HR and payroll teams. Employees who can't bridge the gap turn to expensive short-term credit, making the cycle worse.

PaidNow breaks this cycle — at no cost to you.

How it works for your business

1. Connect payroll

We integrate with your existing payroll system. No changes to your pay cycle or processes.

2. Employees enrol

Staff download the PaidNow app and link to your company using an invite code.

3. Wages tracked live

As employees work, their earned balance updates in real time.

4. Auto reconciliation

At month-end, withdrawals are reconciled automatically with payroll. Zero admin for you.

  • Reduce turnover — employees with financial flexibility stay longer

Not a loan. Not credit.

PaidNow is fundamentally different from payday lenders or salary advance products. Employees access wages they have already earned. There is no interest, no credit check, and no debt created.

This means no risk to your business and no financial liability. PaidNow simply accelerates access to money your employees have already worked for.
